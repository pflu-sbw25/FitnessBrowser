Fitness BLAST Example

For a specified protein sequence, Fitness BLAST shows homologs that have fitness data.

Short Results

The short result shows up to two hits. It always includes the top hit with fitness data (if there is one). It may show another hit if the top hit does not have a significant phenotype or cofitness but another hit does.
